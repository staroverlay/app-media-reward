# Media Reward

Trigger media files based on Twitch channel rewards.

### Features
- **Reward Mapping**: Assign specific videos, images, or audio clips to your Twitch channel points.
- **Multiple Play Modes**: 
  - **Queue**: Alerts wait their turn (sequential).
  - **Only Once**: Ignores new alerts while one is playing.
  - **Render All**: Overlays everything as it arrives.
- **Full Customization**: Adjust volume per reward and change display aspects (Fill or Fit).
